A18 chip. Super fast. Super slim.

Introducing A18, a specially selected chip that is two generations ahead of the A16 Bionic chip of iPhone 15. It enables next-level camera features such as Photographic Styles and the camera controller. The chip is extremely efficient, so the battery does not last longer.

In case of emergency, iPhone comes to your rescue.

iPhone has safety features that can save lives. If you don't have a mobile network or WiFi and you can't call 911, you can use your iPhone to send emergency services text messages via satellite.
